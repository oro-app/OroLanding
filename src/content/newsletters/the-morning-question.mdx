Most mornings do not need a fully original outfit. They need a good answer to a simple question: what does today ask of me?

Maybe it asks for ease. Maybe polish. Maybe movement. Maybe something that survives weather, transit, meetings, errands, and an unplanned dinner.

The right outfit starts by naming the day clearly.

## Occasion is more than a place

Occasion includes where you are going, but also how you want to feel while you are there. Comfortable can still be considered. Practical can still be beautiful.

When the question is better, the outfit usually gets better too.
