The hardest outfits often live between categories.

Not fully cold. Not exactly warm. A coffee that might become dinner. A workday that might become a walk. Transitional dressing asks for clothes that can change their mind without falling apart.

That is where defaults matter.

## Build around the removable piece

Start with the layer you can take off and still like the outfit underneath. A cardigan, overshirt, light jacket, or vest can carry the first impression, but the base should not feel abandoned when the temperature changes.

Good in-between dressing is less about prediction and more about forgiveness.
