Oro starts from a simple belief: getting dressed should feel personal before it feels optimized.

Most style tools begin by asking you to become someone else. New references, new rules, new things to buy. We are interested in the opposite direction. Your closet already contains signals about what you reach for, what you avoid, what makes you feel more like yourself, and what needs a little help coming together.

## The wardrobe is the interface

The most useful styling system is not a feed of perfect outfits. It is a way to understand the pieces you already own, the taste you already have, and the moments you are dressing for.

That is the shape Oro is moving toward: a wardrobe that can reason with you, not over you.
